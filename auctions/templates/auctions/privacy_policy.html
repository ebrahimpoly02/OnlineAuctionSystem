Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

1. Introduction

Bidfinity ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring compliance with the Kingdom of Bahrain's Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL) No. 30 of 2018. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of personal information:

  • Account Information: First name, last name, username, email address, phone number (optional)
  • Auction Information: Listings you create, bids you place, watchlist items
  • Transaction Information: Purchase history, payment details (processed securely)
  • Communication: Messages between buyers and sellers, customer support inquiries
  • Technical Information: IP address, browser type, device information (for security purposes)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your account
  • To facilitate auction listings and bidding
  • To process transactions and send notifications
  • To communicate with you about your account and transactions
  • To improve our platform and user experience
  • To comply with legal obligations under Bahraini law
  • To prevent fraud and ensure platform security

4. Legal Basis for Processing (PDPL Compliance)

Under Bahrain's PDPL, we process your personal data based on:

  • Consent: You provide explicit consent when registering and using our services
  • Contract Performance: Processing is necessary to fulfill our services to you
  • Legal Obligation: Compliance with Bahraini laws and regulations

5. Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Password encryption using PBKDF2-SHA256 hashing algorithm
  • HTTPS/SSL encryption for data transmission
  • Secure cloud storage on AWS infrastructure located in Bahrain (me-south-1 region)
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls limiting employee access to personal data

Your data is stored within the Kingdom of Bahrain and is not transferred outside the country without your explicit consent.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as necessary to provide services. When you delete your account, we permanently delete your personal information within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

7. Your Rights Under PDPL

Under Bahrain's Personal Data Protection Law, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Right to Deletion: Delete your account and personal data (via "Delete Account" button)
  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your personal data
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing at any time

8. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With Other Users: Your username and seller ratings are visible to other platform users
  • Service Providers: AWS for hosting, email service providers for notifications
  • Legal Requirements: When required by Bahraini law or legal process
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (with notice to users)

9. Cookies and Tracking

We use essential cookies to maintain your login session and ensure platform functionality. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies without your explicit consent.

10. Children's Privacy

Our platform is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we discover such collection, we will promptly delete the information.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify users of significant changes via email or platform notification.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your PDPL rights, please contact us at:

Email: bidfinityauction@gmail.com
Data Protection Officer: Available upon request

By using Bidfinity, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and consent to the collection, use, and processing of your personal information as described herein.
